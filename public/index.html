Chat Wallet

A chat room app that lets you keep in touch with your hobbies.

Chat wallet gives users the opportunity to find like-minded individuals to discuss ideas and collaborate on projects, and or seek help with a current project. Chat rooms range from career based discussions to programming languages, hobbies and beyond. Simply register an account via our login portal and you will be free to roam the chat rooms and find one that suits your needs or wants. Registration only requires a username, password, and an e-mail. E-mails will only be used to update users on the incoming changes for the application, no sales gimmicks or ad promotions, guaranteed!
